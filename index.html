ClickerQuest is based on similar games, such as Clicker Heroes, CivClicker, Cookie Clicker, etc. It's a work-in-progress, so check back regularly for updates, and please send in any bug reports or feature requests!

Developed by J. Reel.

Starting Out:

Click the big 'ol [Quest] button a few times to build up some Gold, then hire your first hero when you can afford to. Your hero will Quest for you, accumulating a small amount of Gold each second while Questing, plus a larger Gold reward at the end of the Quest.

Planned Features:

  • After hiring your first hero, clicking the Quest button will instead "boost" your currently-questing hero(es) in some way.
  • Questing will involve random enemy encounters, thereby making the hero stats (defense, offense, etc.) actually mean something.
  • Heroes will be somewhat random on each playthrough, including name, portrait, and stats (within a certain range).
  • Quest rewards will have a chance of including helpful Items.

Technical Stuff:

This game uses emoji (included in Unicode 7.0) to display some icons. If the symbols don't show up for you, it may mean that you're:

  1. not running the latest version of your browser, or
  2. lacking an installed font which supports emoji, or
  3. using some flavor of Linux which lacks emoji support.
If you're interested, you might try resolving the situation by installing the EmojiOne font.
